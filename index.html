Deep Learning Engineer

Groningen, Netherlands

I’m a Machine Learning Engineer specializing in deep learning and computer vision applications. I recently completed my MSc in Computing Science at the University of Groningen, focusing on Intelligent Systems & Visual Computing. My thesis delved into contrastive self-supervised learning for predicting outcomes of patients with oropharyngeal squamous cell carcinoma (OPSCC), leveraging solely their diagnostic imaging tests. This research was conducted under the supervision of Prof. Jiapan Guo in collaboration with the University Medical Center Groningen (UMCG).

Previously, I worked as a Data Scientist at Vodafone Greece, focusing on propensity machine learning models to optimize customer journey actions.
